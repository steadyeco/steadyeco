Portal Features

What You'll Have Access To

Live Status

Live Job Status

See exactly where your project is: Booked, On Site, Complete, or Paid. Updated in real time as work progresses.

Financials

Invoice History

Access all your invoices — current, paid, and overdue. Download or print any invoice directly from the portal.

History

Project Timeline

A full history of every job we've completed on your property or site. Filter by date, service type, or status.

Quotes

Quote Status

Track outstanding quotes and approve or decline work directly through the portal.

Site Records

Photo Reports

Before-and-after site photos uploaded directly to your job record by our crew.

Compliance

Document Library

Compliance documents, SWMS, insurance certificates, and project reports — stored and accessible.

Access Tiers

Three Types of Client Access

Landholder

Landholder Access

Individual property owners. View jobs, invoices, and project photos for your property. Read-only access.

Council / Government

Council / Government Access

Designated procurement officers and project managers. View all active contracts, compliance documents, and invoices. Shared-team access available.

Carbon Farming

Carbon Farming Client

ACCU project participants. View ERF project progress, MERI reports, planting records, and carbon estimation updates.
