About Us

This Trade Is
In Our Blood

Steady Eco is a Cairns-based landscaping, tree services, and blade sharpening business built on 7 years of hands-on arborist experience across Far North Queensland.

This business is dedicated to my father, who gave me this trade from the age of 15 and ran TRIMSNQ with the kind of skill and dedication that FNQ operators knew and trusted. His love for arboriculture lives on in everything we do.

We started with blade sharpening and grew into full-service landscaping and tree work because the equipment, the knowledge, and the demand are all here. Whether you need your yard maintained, your trees sorted, or your chipper blades back to factory spec — we're the call to make.

Our long-term vision is large-scale environmental restoration across FNQ — reef catchment work, revegetation, and land regeneration. Every job today is groundwork for that future.
