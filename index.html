About Us

This Trade Is
In Our Blood

Steady Eco is a Cairns-based business built on years of hands-on experience in arboriculture and vegetation management across Far North Queensland.

This business is dedicated to my father, who gave me this trade from the age of 15 and ran TRIMSNQ with the kind of skill and dedication that FNQ operators knew and trusted. His love for arboriculture lives on in every blade we sharpen.

We started with blade sharpening because we have the machinery, the knowledge, and a clear gap in the local market. If you're running woodchipping equipment in FNQ and your blades are dulling faster than your schedule allows — we're the call to make.
