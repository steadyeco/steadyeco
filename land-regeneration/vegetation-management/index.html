What We Do

Vegetation Management
Services Include:

We provide comprehensive vegetation management services for councils, road authorities, utilities, and land managers across the FNQ region. Our equipment is suited to tropical conditions and FNQ terrain — from coastal flats to tableland roads.

  • ✓ Roadside vegetation clearing and slashing
  • ✓ Mulching and biomass management
  • ✓ Sight-line clearing for road safety
  • ✓ Council reserve and parkland maintenance
  • ✓ Pre-construction clearing and site preparation
  • ✓ Post-clearing biomass removal and disposal
  • ✓ Cyclone and storm damage vegetation management
  • ✓ Annual and programmatic maintenance contracts
  • ✓ Emergency response vegetation clearance
Who This Is For

The clients we work with

FNQ Councils

Cairns, Cassowary Coast, Douglas, Tablelands, Mareeba, Cook, Hinchinbrook. Roadside clearing, reserve maintenance, and programmatic vegetation contracts across the FNQROC region.

Government Agencies

Queensland Department of Transport and Main Roads, Department of Environment, utilities, and state-managed land. Compliant with government procurement and safety requirements.

Private Landholders

Pastoral properties, rural landholders, and developers requiring vegetation management for access tracks, firebreaks, property maintenance, or pre-development clearing.

Equipment

Built for FNQ Conditions

Our equipment is purpose-selected for tropical Far North Queensland — heavy rainfall, dense tropical regrowth, and remote access tracks that standard metropolitan equipment can't handle.

Two trucks and two wood chippers provide both transport capacity and on-site processing capability. We can clear, chip, and remove biomass in a single mobilisation — reducing cost and site disruption for councils.

Service Area

Mobilising From Cairns

Operating out of Cairns, we can mobilise to any location within the Far North Queensland region. We work across all 7 FNQROC councils and can quote for projects throughout the region.

For larger programmatic contracts, we can establish temporary site operations in remote or regional locations within FNQ.
