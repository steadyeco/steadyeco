About the Business

Who We Are

Steady Eco Land Regeneration is FNQ's dedicated ecological restoration contractor. We specialise in vegetation management, native revegetation, weed control, erosion control, carbon farming, and government contract delivery across Far North Queensland. Based in Cairns and operating across all FNQROC council areas.

Scope of Work

Six Core Services

Service 01

Vegetation Management

Roadside, land-clearing follow-up, and council vegetation programs. Equipment: mulchers, slashers, brushcutters.

Service 02

Native Revegetation

MERI-compliant planting programs with native FNQ species. ACCU-eligible revegetation projects.

Service 03

Weed Control

Chemical and manual weed management. Invasive species programs aligned to council biosecurity plans.

Service 04

Erosion Control

Bioengineering, sediment management, waterway restoration. Reef water quality program eligible.

Service 05

Carbon Farming

ACCU methodology: Human-Induced Regeneration (HIR) and Environmental Planting. ERF-registered project development.

Service 06

Government Contracting

Tender-ready for all FNQ council and Queensland government ecological restoration contracts.

Available when the project is ready."
