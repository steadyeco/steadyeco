Before You Enquire

Frequently Asked

Do you work across all of FNQ?

Yes. We service all 7 FNQROC council areas from our Cairns base. Remote sites such as Cape York attract a mobilisation surcharge — ask us for details when you enquire.

Can councils engage you directly without a tender?

For projects under $200,000, most FNQ councils can engage suppliers directly. Download our capability statement or contact us — we'll guide you through the process.

Do you have a capability statement?

Yes. View and print our capability statement online at /land-regeneration/capability-statement, or mention it in your enquiry and we'll send it directly.

What information should I include in my enquiry?

Site location, approximate area (hectares), what needs to be done, and any deadlines or funding constraints. The more detail, the faster we can respond with a useful quote.
