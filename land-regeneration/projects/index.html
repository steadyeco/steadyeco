Scope of Work

What a Steady Eco Project Looks Like

01

Roadside Vegetation Management

Council roadside programs. Mulching, slashing, and vegetation clearing along public roads and infrastructure corridors.

02

Post-Clearing Revegetation

Native species planting following land clearing. MERI-compliant programs with ongoing monitoring and reporting.

03

Invasive Weed Removal

Property-wide or corridor-based weed programs. Manual and chemical treatment. Species-specific programs for Cairns and Wet Tropics weed priorities.

04

Waterway Restoration

Erosion control and riparian revegetation. Reef water quality eligible. Bioengineering and sediment management.

05

Carbon Farming Setup

ERF project development and ACCU methodology selection. Human-Induced Regeneration and Environmental Planting projects.

06

Government Ecological Works

Council and Queensland government ecological restoration contracts. Tender-ready delivery with compliance documentation.
