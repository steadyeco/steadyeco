What We Do

Weed Control
Services Include

FNQ's tropical climate makes invasive weed species one of the greatest threats to native ecosystems. Effective control requires local knowledge, the right methods for each species, and a commitment to follow-through — not a single treatment, but a managed program.

  • ✓ Mechanical weed removal and slashing
  • ✓ Targeted chemical weed treatment (licensed)
  • ✓ Integrated weed management programs
  • ✓ Invasive species mapping and reporting
  • ✓ Pond weed and aquatic vegetation management
  • ✓ Bush regeneration to SERA national standards
  • ✓ Post-clearing weed suppression and monitoring
  • ✓ Council reserve and public land weed programs
  • ✓ Riparian (waterway) weed control
"A weed is only a plant in the wrong place. Restoring what belongs is the work."

Our Methods

Integrated Weed
Management

Mechanical Methods

Physical Removal

Chippers, slashers, and hand tools for large-scale clearing, roadside management, and species where chemical methods are restricted — near waterways, organic properties, and sensitive areas.

Chemical Methods

Targeted Treatment

Species-specific herbicide application by licensed operators. Full compliance with Queensland chemical application regulations and safety requirements. Every treatment documented.

Integrated Management

Combined Approach

The most effective long-term solution — mechanical and chemical methods in sequence, followed by revegetation to prevent reinvasion. Recommended for council contracts and large properties.
