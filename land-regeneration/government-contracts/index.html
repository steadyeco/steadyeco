How to Engage Us

Four Paths to Working Together

Path 01

Direct Quote

For projects under $200,000, councils can engage us directly without a formal tender. Contact us, describe your project, and we'll quote within 3 business days.

Path 02

Panel Pre-Qualification

Download our capability statement and add us to your supplier list before a tender is released. We can be pre-qualified on FNQROC, TenderLink, and VendorPanel.

Path 03

Tender Response

We respond to all relevant FNQ council and Queensland government tenders for vegetation management, ecological restoration, revegetation, and weed control work.

Path 04

Program Partnership

For multi-year vegetation management programs, we are open to preferred supplier arrangements. Contact us to discuss annual contract structures.

Where to Find Steady Eco

Regional Procurement

FNQROC

Far North Queensland Regional Organisation of Councils — the procurement body for all 12 FNQ councils. We are registered and seek panel placement for ecological restoration work.

State & National Tenders

TenderLink

Queensland government and council tenders published through TenderLink. We monitor and respond to all relevant vegetation management, revegetation, and ecological restoration tenders in FNQ.

Queensland Government

VendorPanel

Queensland government procurement platform for direct-to-supplier quotations. We are registered and available for invitation-to-quote processes from Queensland agencies.
