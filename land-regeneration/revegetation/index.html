ACCU Scheme

Landholders Can Earn Income
from Revegetation

Australia's ACCU (Australian Carbon Credit Unit) scheme allows landholders to earn income by revegetating cleared land. Each tonne of CO₂ absorbed by new native vegetation generates one ACCU — which can be sold to Australian companies meeting their emissions reduction obligations.

We act as the on-ground delivery contractor — planting, managing, and documenting the revegetation to Clean Energy Regulator standards.

Step 01

Eligibility Assessment

We assess your land for ACCU scheme eligibility — location, land history, vegetation type, and area. Most cleared FNQ land qualifies.

Step 02

Project Registration

We assist with Clean Energy Regulator project registration. Once registered, the project earns credits for 25 years.

Step 03

We Deliver & Document

We plant, manage, and provide all required reporting and monitoring documentation for the credit auditing process.
